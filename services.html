Our Approach to Couples Therapy

At MindWell, our couples therapists are trained in multiple evidence-based approaches, including:

Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT)

Focuses on strengthening the attachment bond between partners and creating new patterns of emotional connection.

Gottman Method

Based on 40+ years of research on what makes relationships succeed or fail, focusing on building friendship, managing conflict, and creating shared meaning.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Helps couples identify and change negative thought patterns that impact their relationship interactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does insurance cover mental health services?

Most insurance plans provide some level of coverage for mental health services. Coverage varies by plan and provider. Under the Mental Health Parity Act, insurance companies must provide comparable coverage for mental health as they do for physical health.

What if my insurance doesn't cover the service I need?

If your insurance doesn't cover a specific service or if you're uninsured, we offer sliding scale fees based on income and financial need. Our goal is to make quality care accessible to everyone.

How do I know what my out-of-pocket costs will be?

Our billing team can provide a cost estimate before your first appointment based on your insurance coverage or our sliding scale. We believe in transparency about costs and will discuss all financial aspects before treatment begins.

Do you offer superbills for out-of-network reimbursement?

Yes, we can provide superbills that you can submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement if we are out-of-network with your provider.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, checks, and electronic payments. Payment is typically collected at the time of service unless other arrangements have been made.

Need help with insurance or billing?

Our dedicated billing specialists are here to help you navigate insurance and payment options.
