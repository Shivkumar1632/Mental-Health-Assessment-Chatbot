Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, approach, and how to get started.

Therapy can benefit anyone looking to improve their mental health, work through challenges, or develop better coping strategies. If you're experiencing persistent feelings of sadness, anxiety, or stress that interfere with your daily life, therapy may be particularly helpful. Our initial consultation can help determine if our services are a good fit for your needs.

Our therapy sessions range from $90-$150 per session, depending on the type of therapy and the therapist's experience level. We accept many insurance plans and offer sliding scale fees for those with financial need. During your initial consultation, we'll discuss payment options and verify your insurance benefits if applicable.

The duration of therapy varies based on individual needs and goals. Some clients benefit from short-term therapy (8-12 sessions) focused on specific issues, while others prefer longer-term support. Your therapist will work with you to develop a treatment plan and regularly assess progress. Many clients begin with weekly sessions and adjust frequency as they progress.

Research shows that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person therapy for many mental health concerns. Online therapy offers additional benefits like convenience, accessibility, and comfort of receiving care from your own space. Our platform uses secure, HIPAA-compliant technology to ensure confidentiality. Some clients may still prefer in-person sessions, and we can discuss which format might work best for you.

Getting started is simple! You can sign up for an account on our website, complete a brief assessment about your needs and preferences, and we'll match you with appropriate therapists. You can then schedule a free 15-minute consultation to find the right fit before booking your first full session. If you have questions at any point, our support team is available to help guide you through the process.
